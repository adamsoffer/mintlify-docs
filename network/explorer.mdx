The [Livepeer Explorer](https://explorer.livepeer.org) is the network's transparency layer. It gives you a live view of every active orchestrator, all delegated stake, and aggregate network statistics — without requiring any technical knowledge to use. Whether you're a delegator managing your stake or an orchestrator monitoring your node, the Explorer is where you track your participation in the network.

<Note>
  The Explorer is separate from the Developer Dashboard. The **Developer Dashboard** is for developers building with the Livepeer API — managing API keys, tracking usage, and exploring pipelines. The **Explorer** is for network participants — delegators managing stake and orchestrators monitoring their node performance.
</Note>

## What you can do in the Explorer

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Browse orchestrators" icon="list">
    View every active orchestrator on the network. For each one, see their uptime history, performance metrics, commission rate, total delegated stake, and routing weight.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delegate and manage stake" icon="coins">
    Connect your Ethereum wallet to delegate LPT to an orchestrator, redelegate to a different one, or begin the unbonding process to withdraw your stake.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Claim rewards" icon="hand-holding-dollar">
    Accumulated fee shares and LPT inflation rewards don't transfer automatically — use the Explorer to claim them to your wallet whenever you choose.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track network stats" icon="chart-bar">
    Monitor total staked LPT, the number of active orchestrators, total jobs served, current inflation rate, and round history at the protocol level.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Monitor your orchestrator" icon="server">
    If you run an orchestrator, the Explorer shows your node's stake weight, performance score, active job count, and fee earnings in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Governance" icon="landmark">
    Access on-chain governance participation, view active proposals, and cast your vote as an LPT holder.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## For delegators

The Explorer is your primary interface for staking activity. After you [delegate your LPT](/network/staking-delegation), return here to:

- Check your current delegated stake and the orchestrator you're backing
- View your accumulated fee rewards and inflation rewards
- Claim rewards to your wallet
- Switch to a different orchestrator if performance degrades

## For orchestrators

After you [register your node](/network/run-orchestrator), the Explorer shows:

- Your current routing weight relative to other orchestrators
- Your stake total, including self-delegation and community delegators
- Your performance score and uptime history
- Fees earned per round

Use the Explorer alongside your local node logs to get a complete picture of your operation's health and earnings.
