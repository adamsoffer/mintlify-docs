Delegation lets you put your LPT to work without operating a node. You stake your tokens toward an orchestrator you trust, earn a share of the fees developers pay for GPU inference, and collect LPT inflation rewards — all from your Ethereum wallet. The process is non-custodial: you keep ownership of your tokens while they're staked.

<Note>
  Delegation is for LPT holders who want to participate in network economics. You do **not** need to delegate, hold LPT, or interact with the protocol in any way to use the Livepeer inference API as a developer.
</Note>

## Why delegate

When a developer pays fees for AI inference on the Livepeer network, those fees flow to orchestrators and their delegators. As a delegator you earn:

- **A share of developer fees** — a portion of the ETH or stablecoins paid for every inference job routed to your chosen orchestrator.
- **LPT inflation rewards** — the protocol mints new LPT over time (similar to block rewards in Bitcoin and Ethereum) and distributes it to active participants in proportion to their stake.

Delegation is also how the network signals quality. Orchestrators with more delegated stake receive more job routing weight, so your delegation shapes which operators the network trusts most.

## Staking mechanics

| Concept | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Lock period** | Tokens are locked while staked. Unbonding takes approximately 7 days. |
| **Commission** | Each orchestrator sets its own commission rate on rewards earned. |
| **Slashing risk** | There is no slashing risk for delegators. If an orchestrator misbehaves, you can redelegate; you don't lose your principal. |
| **Claiming rewards** | Accumulated rewards must be claimed manually through the Explorer. |

## How to delegate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get LPT">
    Purchase LPT on a supported exchange:
    [Binance](https://www.binance.com/en/trade/LPT_USDT) · [Coinbase](https://www.coinbase.com/price/livepeer) · [Kraken](https://www.kraken.com/prices/livepeer) · [Uniswap](https://app.uniswap.org/tokens/ethereum/0x58b6a8a3302369daec383334672404ee733ab239) · [OKX](https://www.okx.com/trade-spot/lpt-usdt)

    Transfer the LPT to a self-custodial Ethereum wallet (MetaMask or any compatible wallet).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Livepeer Explorer">
    Go to [explorer.livepeer.org](https://explorer.livepeer.org) and connect your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse orchestrators">
    Review the list of active orchestrators. For each one you can see:
    - Performance metrics and uptime history
    - Commission rate on fees and inflation rewards
    - Total delegated stake and routing weight

    Choose an orchestrator with a consistent performance record and a commission rate you're comfortable with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delegate">
    Click **Delegate** on your chosen orchestrator, enter the amount of LPT you want to stake, and confirm the transaction in your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start earning">
    Your stake is active immediately. Rewards accumulate each round. Return to the Explorer to claim them whenever you like.
  </Step>
</Steps>
