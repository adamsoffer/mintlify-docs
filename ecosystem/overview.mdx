Livepeer is an open network. Independent teams build products — called **demand-side solutions** — on top of its GPU inference and transcoding capabilities, packaging them into experiences for developers, creators, and end users. The applications below represent the range of what builders are shipping today, from generative AI video pipelines to decentralized social streaming infrastructure.

## Solutions built on Livepeer

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Daydream" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="https://daydream.live">

## Categories

Solutions in the ecosystem span several categories:

- **AI Video** — real-time generative video, style transfer, object detection
- **Streaming** — live video ingest, transcoding, VOD, and delivery
- **Agents** — LLM and vision inference for autonomous agent systems
- **Community** — creator tools, reward engines, and audience platforms
- **Decentralized social** — federated video infrastructure built on open protocols
